How to Use BrainScan AI

  1. Prepare a clear image of a single brain MRI scan (JPG, PNG or JPEG.).
  2. Click Browse Files or drag and drop your MRI image into the upload area.
  3. Preview your image and confirm it is a brain MRI scan when prompted.
  4. Click "Analyze Brain Scan" to start the AI analysis.
  5. Wait for the results. Please do not refresh or close the page during analysis.

Note: Only brain MRI scan images are supported. Other image types may give inaccurate results.
