About BrainScan AI

Advancing brain tumor detection through artificial intelligence

Our Mission

At BrainScan AI, we're dedicated to improving the accuracy and speed of brain tumor detection through advanced machine learning technologies. Our goal is to provide healthcare professionals with powerful tools that assist in early diagnosis, leading to better patient outcomes.

The Technology

Our brain tumor detection system utilizes a convolutional neural network (CNN) trained on thousands of MRI scans. The model can identify four categories with high precision:

  • Glioma Tumors
  • Meningioma Tumors
  • Pituitary Tumors
  • No Tumor (healthy brain scans)

The model achieves an accuracy rate of over 98% in controlled testing environments, comparing favorably to the diagnostic accuracy of experienced radiologists.

Research Background

Our technology is built upon research in medical imaging analysis using deep learning techniques. The CNN architecture was developed specifically for MRI scan analysis, with attention to the unique characteristics of brain tissue and tumor manifestations.

The training dataset includes diverse patient demographics and varying tumor sizes, locations, and stages to ensure robust performance across real-world clinical scenarios.

Data Privacy

We take data privacy extremely seriously. All images uploaded to our system are:

  • Processed entirely on secure servers
  • Never stored beyond the analysis period
  • Not used for further model training without explicit consent
  • Protected with encryption standards

Medical Disclaimer

BrainScan AI is designed to be a diagnostic aid for healthcare professionals, not a replacement for professional medical judgment. All analysis results should be reviewed and confirmed by qualified medical personnel before making clinical decisions.

Our Team

Our interdisciplinary team brings together expertise in neurology, radiology, machine learning, and software development. With backgrounds from leading medical institutions and technology companies, we're committed to creating tools that make a meaningful difference in patient care.
